About Us

We are a Mama centric company that was founded on the basis of connecting mothers for collaboration, socialization and education. Using our matching criteria, Mamas are recommended to each other, simply, safely, and privately. Whether it is lifestyle, a childs age, or after school activities, a parent will find a connection that fits their needs. In other words, Mamas and Papas create their own tribe! We believe that the world will be a healthier and happier place, with parent tribes everywhere.
